    🏠 Home Care Routine
      🥗 Diet & Nutrition Tips
      • 🥩
        High-protein diet

        Eggs, lentils, fish and lean meats support keratin production and hair growth.

      • 🫐
        Antioxidant-rich foods

        Berries, spinach and broccoli combat scalp inflammation.

      • 🐟
        Omega-3 fatty acids

        Salmon, walnuts and flaxseed help reduce scalp dryness and irritation.

      • 💧
        Stay hydrated

        Drink 8–10 glasses of water daily to keep the scalp moisturized.

      • 🚫
        Avoid processed sugars

        High glycemic foods can worsen inflammatory scalp conditions.

      💆 Scalp Care Habits
      • 🚿
        Gentle washing

        Use lukewarm water and a gentle, sulfate-free shampoo 2–3 times per week.

      • 🎯
        Scalp massage

        4 minutes of daily fingertip massage improves circulation and promotes growth.

      • 🔥
        Limit heat styling

        Avoid blow dryers, flat irons and curlers. Air-dry whenever possible.

      • 🧴
        Moisturize regularly

        Apply a lightweight scalp serum or oil (rosemary, argan) to prevent dryness.

      🧘 Stress & Wellness
      • 🧘
        Daily meditation

        Even 10 minutes reduces cortisol, which is linked to hair loss and inflammation.

      • 🏃
        Regular exercise

        30 minutes of aerobic exercise improves scalp blood flow and reduces stress hormones.

      • 😴
        Quality sleep

        7–9 hours on a silk pillowcase reduces friction and supports hair repair.

      • 🌞
        Sun protection

        Use SPF 30+ on exposed scalp or wear a hat when outdoors.
